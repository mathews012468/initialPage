Scavenger Hunt

The prize is a book. Follow the clues and you will find it.

There are many named grass-filled plots and triangles in the Maspeth area. All of the clues in this hunt will take you to one of these plots or triangles. The next clue will always be at a link that starts with "tinyurl.com/", usually followed by the answer to the clue. Any deviation from this pattern will be noted in the clue itself.

As an example, suppose that there is a clue that says "A part of a ship." The only named triangle in the Maspeth area that fits the description (to the best of my knowledge) is "Hull Triangle". This means that the next clue would be found at tinyurl.com/hull, and the clue at that link might reference a physical feature of that triangle.

Clue:

First modern heliocentric astronomer.
